Specialization - Coursera

This is a four course specialization which lay the foundation for all concepts in Self-Driving Cars.



Introduction to Self-Driving Cars

This course laid the foundation for various segments incorporated in the development of an autonomous vehicle. Learned the various terminology, design considerations and safety assessment of self-driving cars.

State Estimation and Localization for Self-Driving Cars

Introduced to different sensors and their use for state estimation and localization in self-driving car. Developed models for localisation sensors such as IMU and GPS and applied Extended and Unscented Kalman Filter for the estimation problem.

Visual Perception for Self-Driving Cars

Derived a pin-hole camera model and Performed intrinsic and extrinsic calculations on it. Performed static and dynamic object detection using deep learning. Applied semantic segmentation for the estimation of the drivable surface.

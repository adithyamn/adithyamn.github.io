RoboCUBES - Modular Reconfigurable Robot System

❮ ❯

Project Overview

What if a robot could change its shape based on what it needs to do? That’s exactly what RoboCUBES aimed to achieve! This project involved building a modular reconfigurable robot that detects its own configuration and adapts accordingly. Developed a hardware addressing system that allowed these robots to autonomously identify their configuration—so they could morph into different forms based on the task. This work led to a patent on the hardware addressing system, watch the Video!
